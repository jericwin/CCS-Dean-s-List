Welcome to the College of Computer Studies

The College of Computer Studies (CCS) provides world-class education in Information Technology, Computer Science, and Information Systems. Our programs focus on innovation, practical skills, and ethical computing to prepare students for leadership in the tech industry.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Learn software development, networking, animation, and IT management skills to tackle real-world tech challenges.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Master algorithms, programming languages, AI, and software engineering principles to innovate and solve complex problems.

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

Combine business knowledge and technology to design and manage enterprise systems effectively and efficiently.
